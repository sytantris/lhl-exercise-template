Goal

In this exercise you'll learn to use EJS templates and create their corresponding routes in Express. Learning to use a templating language and understanding how variables are passed and utilized is extremely important, and will serve to further your TinyApp progress.

Objectives

  • Learn about EJS templates.
  • Use EJS templates to display data dynamically.
  • Create routes that render EJS templates.
  • Use partials to encapsulate repetitive markup.
  • Make progress on your TinyApp.

Summary

By the end of this exercise, you should have:

  • Learned the basics of EJS templating.
  • Created some EJS templates and written corresponding routes to render them.
  • Passed variables to your templates and accessed them with EJS tags.
  • Written and included partials to encapsulate repetitive markup.
  • Created your 'urls_index' and 'urls_show' templates for your TinyApp.

If you feel like you are unclear on any of these objectives, make sure to ask a mentor for assistance! Don't forget to commit your work.
